Isabelle Otito Analo

This is my website for IML300 class. Isabelle Analo is a Nigerian American multimedia visual artist based in Pittsburgh.

Exploring themes of Black youth and Afrofuturism, her work aims to create a physical representation of the dreams of young black kids everywhere.

Analo is proficient in Adobe photoshop, lightroom, bridge, illustrator, and premier, as well as Blender.

She is also skilled in graphic design, photography, and cinematography.
